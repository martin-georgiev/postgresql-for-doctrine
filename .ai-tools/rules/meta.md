# Meta

## Propose Rule Updates After Repeated Corrections
When you notice repeated corrections or extensive back-and-forth on the same topic during a session, proactively propose updates to `.ai-tools/rules` to capture the learnings. This prevents the same issues from recurring in future sessions.
